The Challenge

Manual Errors

Paper-based attendance is prone to human error and data entry fatigue.

Proxy Attendance

Students marking attendance for friends who are not physically present.

Lack of Insights

Admins cannot easily analyze real-time attendance trends or academic progress.

The Solution

An integrated digital ecosystem that combines biometric security with real-time location tracking.

Biometric Trust

FIDO2/WebAuthn standard for cryptographic fingerprint verification.

Geofenced Safety

Ensures attendance is only marked within the authorized campus radius.

Dynamic QR

Time-sensitive session tokens that expire to prevent misuse.

Biometric (FIDO2) Privacy

What we DO NOT store

No Fingerprint Images. No Biometric Templates. Privacy is guaranteed by hardware.

What we DO store

Public Keys and Credential IDs used for cryptographic signature verification.

Complies with modern FIDO2 standards, making the portal virtually unhackable via traditional replay attacks.
